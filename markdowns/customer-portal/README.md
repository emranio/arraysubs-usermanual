# Customer Portal

Use this section when you want to understand the **customer-facing side** of ArraySubs inside **WooCommerce → My Account**.

This is where customers review their subscriptions, pay pending renewal invoices, and use the self-service actions you chose to expose.

## What belongs here

These pages explain how customers can:

- open the **My Subscriptions** list and the single-subscription view
- understand subscription status, billing details, related orders, refunds, and notes
- use supported self-service actions such as plan change, cancellation, skip, pause, and resume
- manage payment and shipping details when the relevant module or gateway supports it
- access Pro-only customer pages such as **My Features** and **Store Credit**

## Start here

- [My Subscriptions and View Subscription](./my-subscriptions-and-view-subscription.md) — the main customer pages, what they show, and how pending renewal invoices are paid.
- [Customer Self-Service Actions](./customer-self-service-actions.md) — plan changes, cancellation flow, skip, pause, and resume behavior.
- [Payment, Shipping, and Pro Portal Pages](./payment-shipping-and-pro-portal-pages.md) — payment-method management, auto-renew toggle, shipping updates, My Features, and Store Credit.
- [Lifecycle States and Renewal Recovery](./lifecycle-states-and-renewal-recovery.md) — scheduled cancellation, on-hold recovery, reactivation behavior, and customer-visible history.

## What does not belong here

This section does **not** document:

- admin subscription editing and support-side actions
- gateway setup and webhook configuration *(Pro)*
- Store Credit admin settings and back-office adjustments *(Pro)*
- Feature Manager setup and entitlement design *(Pro)*
- My Account layout-builder configuration

Those topics belong in their own module guides.

## Recommended reading order

1. Start with [My Subscriptions and View Subscription](./my-subscriptions-and-view-subscription.md).
2. Then read [Customer Self-Service Actions](./customer-self-service-actions.md) to understand what customers can change themselves.
3. Add [Payment, Shipping, and Pro Portal Pages](./payment-shipping-and-pro-portal-pages.md) when your store uses automatic payments, shipping updates, Feature Manager, or Store Credit.
4. Keep [Lifecycle States and Renewal Recovery](./lifecycle-states-and-renewal-recovery.md) nearby for support, QA, and cancellation/recovery review.
