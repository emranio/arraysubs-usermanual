# Manage Members *(Pro)*

Use this section when you need a **customer-by-customer operational view** across subscriptions, WooCommerce spending, addresses, store credit context, and support shortcuts.

This is the merchant-facing member-insight workspace inside the ArraySubs admin app.

## What belongs here

These pages explain how to:

- open the **Manage Members** screen from the main ArraySubs admin menu or from shortcut links across WooCommerce and ArraySubs
- search for members by name, username, or email
- review the member detail screen, including profile data, roles, addresses, key totals, and impersonation support
- move from a member record into related subscription, order, store-credit, and feature-entitlement workflows

## Start here

- [Access, Search, and Entry Points](./access-search-and-entry-points.md) — learn where the screen appears, who can use it, and how support staff typically jump into a member record.
- [Member Detail Screen](./member-detail-screen.md) — review the profile header, address cards, key statistics, and the Login as Customer workflow.
- [Commerce Overview and Connected Workflows](./commerce-overview-and-connected-workflows.md) — understand the subscription table, purchased-product summary, and links out to Store Credit, orders, and feature review.

## What does not belong here

This section does **not** replace:

- the full subscription-detail guide
- the dedicated Store Credit admin pages *(Pro)*
- WooCommerce Analytics customer reporting
- the customer-facing portal

Manage Members is the **hub view** that helps you reach those workflows faster.

## Recommended reading order

1. Start with [Access, Search, and Entry Points](./access-search-and-entry-points.md).
2. Then review [Member Detail Screen](./member-detail-screen.md) so your team understands what data is visible in the member profile.
3. Use [Commerce Overview and Connected Workflows](./commerce-overview-and-connected-workflows.md) when documenting day-to-day support or success-team processes.
