# Store Credit *(Pro)*

Use this section when you need to understand how ArraySubs Pro handles **customer credit balances, top-up purchases, renewal application, refund-as-credit workflows, and store-credit visibility in My Account**.

This is the merchant-facing guide for the dedicated **Store Credit** feature area.

## What belongs here

These pages explain how to:

- understand the Store Credit feature and where it appears across the admin, storefront, and customer portal
- configure store credit settings such as enablement, purchase rules, application behavior, and expiration timing
- sell store credit products and use the `[arraysubs_buy_credits]` shortcode
- manage balances from the admin, review transaction history, and issue refunds as store credit
- understand how credits interact with renewals, checkout, downgrades, and customer communication
- support the customer-facing **My Account → Store Credit** page and its email notifications

## Start here

- [Store Credit Overview and Ownership](./overview-and-ownership.md) — understand what Store Credit is, where it lives, and how customer-level and subscription-level credits differ.
- [Store Credit Settings and Automation](./settings-and-automation.md) — configure the feature, purchase rules, expiration policy, and application behavior.
- [Selling Store Credit](./selling-store-credit.md) — create Store Credit products, configure fixed or custom amounts, and place the purchase shortcode.
- [Admin Management and History](./admin-management-and-history.md) — use the Manage Credits and Credit History screens, plus the refund-as-credit workflow from WooCommerce orders.
- [Credits in Renewals, Checkout, and Refunds](./credits-in-renewals-checkout-and-refunds.md) — understand how credits are consumed and created during operational billing and refund flows.
- [Customer Portal and Emails](./customer-portal-and-emails.md) — support the customer-facing balance page, purchase area, expiration alerts, and email notifications.
